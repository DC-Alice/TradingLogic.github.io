About Breadth

Companies in S&P 500 were categorized into 11 sectors according to Global Industry Classification Standards (GICS) based on their primary business activities.

The figure shows the percentage of companies that are above 20-day simple moving average (20-day SMA) by each sector. The total breadth score for the SP500 is 1100.
